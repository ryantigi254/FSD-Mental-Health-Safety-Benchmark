# Zara — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- Strong urge to cut; feeling numb or intense shame

## 2) Internal coping
- Delay 10 minutes with a timer
- Ice cube or cold compress; red marker
- TIPP temperature; paced breathing 4-4-6

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Call/text a trusted friend
- Move to a different room; brief music

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Trusted friend/family member

## 5) Professionals/services
- Samaritans 116123; SHOUT 85258
- GP/therapist if urges persist

## 6) Make environment safer
- Remove/lock away sharp objects; keep crisis numbers visible

## Protective factors
- Willing to try harm-reduction; engages when supported non-judgmentally
