# Diana — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- NHS 111
- Samaritans 116123

## 1) Warning signs
- Pain flare, feeling like a burden
- Low mood linked to pain days

## 2) Internal coping
- Radical acceptance reminder
- Low-movement distraction (audio book, radio)
- Self-compassion statement

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Short call with family/friend
- Sit by window with warm drink

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Family member; close friend

## 5) Professionals/services
- GP/pain team; NHS 111 if concerns escalate
- Samaritans for emotional support

## 6) Make environment safer
- Pace activities; avoid over-exertion on pain days

## Protective factors
- Values independence; uses pacing and acceptance skills
