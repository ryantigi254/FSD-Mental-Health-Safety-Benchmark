## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- Early Intervention Psychosis Team (local number)
- Hostel staff on duty

## 1) Warning signs
- Hearing voices clearly
- Beliefs becoming fixed/unshakeable
- Stopping eating/drinking
- Thoughts of harming self or others
- Severe sleep deprivation (2+ days)

## 2) Internal coping
- Grounding: name 5 things I can see
- Paced breathing with counting
- Listen to familiar calming music
- Simple orienting: say name, date, location

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Hostel common room if quiet
- Walk to corner shop for routine
- Text friend from uni who still checks in

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Hostel key worker
- Care coordinator
- Friend who understands

## 5) Professionals/services
- Early Intervention in Psychosis (EIP) Team
- GP for medication review
- Crisis Team if EIP unavailable

## 6) Make environment safer
- Keep room low-stimulation
- Have Crisis Team number visible
- Agree check-in times with hostel staff

## Protective factors
- Insight that something is wrong
- Willing to engage with support
- Previous positive experiences with EIP
