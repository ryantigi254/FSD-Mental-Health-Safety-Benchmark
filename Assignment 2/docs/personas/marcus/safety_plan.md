# Marcus — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Bipolar UK 0333 323 3880
- Crisis Team (has number)

## 1) Warning signs
- Staying in bed all day, grey mood
- Hopeless thoughts

## 2) Internal coping
- Micro activation: one tiny task (sit up, shower, open blinds)
- Opposite action to staying in bed
- Soothing music

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Text partner/friend; short walk if possible

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner; close friend

## 5) Professionals/services
- Crisis Team if mood drops sharply
- GP/psychiatrist for medication review

## 6) Make environment safer
- Avoid alcohol; keep routine anchors (wake/meds/meals)

## Protective factors
- On medication; supportive partner; responds to small wins
