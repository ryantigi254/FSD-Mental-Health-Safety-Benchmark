## 1) Warning signs
- Skipping multiple meals
- Extreme distress about specific body parts
- Complete social withdrawal due to appearance
- New urges to harm appearance (picking, etc.)

## 2) Internal coping
- Self-compassion script: "What would I say to a friend?"
- Opposite Action: leave mirror, put on comfortable clothes
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding when comparison spirals start

## 3) Environment adjustments
- Set social media time limits
- Curate feed to reduce comparison triggers
- Cover or reduce mirrors if checking is compulsive

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Supportive friend who doesn't comment on appearance
- Family member who can redirect appearance comments

## 5) Professionals/services
- GP for referral to body image or eating support
- NHS Talking Therapies
- Beat Eating Disorders helpline

## 6) Protective factors
- Aware of social media impact
- Open to values-based work
- Has supportive peer connections
