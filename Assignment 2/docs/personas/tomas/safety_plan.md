# Tomás — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- NHS 111

## 1) Warning signs
- Anger surge after work conflict, urge to send angry emails

## 2) Internal coping
- STOP skill
- Paced breathing 4-4-6
- Opposite action: step away, water splash, brief stretch

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Walk outside for 5 minutes
- Call a trusted friend/partner

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner or colleague who stays calm

## 5) Professionals/services
- NHS 111 if risk of outburst or health concern

## 6) Make environment safer
- Delay sending emails; draft/save only
- Move away from triggers; unclench fists/shoulders

## Protective factors
- Goal-oriented; willing to use concrete steps
