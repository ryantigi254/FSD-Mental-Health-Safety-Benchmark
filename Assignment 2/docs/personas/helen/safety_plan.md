## Emergency contacts
- 999 (crisis)
- Samaritans 116123
- PANDAS 0808 1961 776
- Midwife direct line

## 1) Warning signs
- Thoughts of harming self or baby
- Complete disconnection from pregnancy
- Unable to care for first child
- Severe appetite/sleep changes

## 2) Internal coping
- Gentle breathing
- Speaking to baby even without feeling
- One small self-care activity daily
- Writing feelings in private journal

## 3) Support contacts
- Husband (understanding)
- One friend who knows situation
- PANDAS helpline

## 4) Professionals
- Midwife for perinatal mental health referral
- Perinatal mental health team
- Health visitor post-birth
- GP

## 5) Cultural considerations
- Privacy from extended family initially
- Non-judgmental support
- Acknowledgment of cultural expectations

## Protective factors
- Engaged with healthcare
- Supportive husband
- History of mild depression (recovered)
- First child healthy and loved
