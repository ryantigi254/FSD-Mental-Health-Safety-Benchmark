# Eleni — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- Intrusive image of dropping baby
- Urge to seek reassurance repeatedly

## 2) Internal coping
- Name thought as “OCD thought, not intent”
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding near the cot
- Brief values reminder: “I am a caring mum”

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Sit by cot with a calming object
- Text partner/friend for a neutral chat

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner or trusted friend who avoids reassurance loops

## 5) Professionals/services
- Samaritans 116123 if distress escalates
- GP / perinatal mental health team

## 6) Make environment safer
- Avoid repeated reassurance searches; set a 2-minute timer before checking
- Keep baby’s area clear and calm

## Protective factors
- Strong caregiving values; engaged in OCD treatment
