# Jordan — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123

## 1) Warning signs
- Flat mood, “what’s the point”, withdrawal

## 2) Internal coping
- Short walk; brief music; grounding 5-4-3-2-1
- One small action (shower, tidy desk)

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Text partner/friend; sit in a public space (café/library)

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner; one trusted friend

## 5) Professionals/services
- Samaritans 116123 if mood worsens
- GP if low mood persists

## 6) Make environment safer
- Avoid isolation when mood drops; limit alcohol

## Protective factors
- Partner involvement; can engage if not pushed
