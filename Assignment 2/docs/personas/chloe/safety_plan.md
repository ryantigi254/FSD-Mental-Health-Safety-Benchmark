# Chloe — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- PANDAS Foundation 0808 1961 776

## 1) Warning signs
- Repeated baby-checking, high anxiety
- Sleep deprivation worsening worry

## 2) Internal coping
- Check-the-facts about current worry
- Quick grounding while baby naps
- Brief self-compassion reminder: “Good enough mum”

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Call partner/friend for a 2-minute check-in
- Sit in a calm room while baby sleeps

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner; trusted friend; health visitor

## 5) Professionals/services
- PANDAS helpline; GP/health visitor

## 6) Make environment safer
- Limit repeated checks; set timers
- Avoid comparing to other parents online

## Protective factors
- Caring parent; willing to ground and reframe
