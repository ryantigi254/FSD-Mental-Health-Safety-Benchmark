# Victor — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Crisis Team (has number)
- Rethink 0808 801 0525

## 1) Warning signs
- Paranoid thoughts (“people watching me”)
- Feeling “things are getting strange”

## 2) Internal coping
- Gentle reality-check: list facts vs fears
- Grounding: 5 senses
- Routine anchor: meal, walk, music

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Call trusted person; sit in a calm room

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Friend/family who understands his plan

## 5) Professionals/services
- Crisis Team; Rethink helpline; GP/psychiatrist

## 6) Make environment safer
- Avoid cannabis/trigger substances; maintain sleep schedule; keep meds routine

## Protective factors
- Stable on meds; proactive about warning signs
