# Mira — Emotional Support Plan

## If distress becomes overwhelming
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- Constant rumination about ex
- Isolating from friends for days
- Increased crying or inability to eat

## 2) Internal coping
- Journal: write unsent letter to process feelings
- Opposite Action: reach out to friend when urge is to isolate
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding when overwhelmed

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Best friend for coffee or call
- Gym or yoga class for physical release
- Library or café for change of scenery

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Close friend who listens without judgment
- Family member for practical support

## 5) Professionals/services
- NHS Talking Therapies (self-refer)
- Private counsellor for relationship-focused sessions

## 6) Make environment safer
- Mute/block ex on social media temporarily
- Remove physical reminders if too painful

## Protective factors
- Strong friendships
- Engages in hobbies
- Willing to process emotions verbally
