# Kai — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Parent/guardian

## 1) Warning signs
- Sensory overload: bright lights, beeps, crowds
- Headache, words hard to process

## 2) Internal coping
- Earphones on; quick exit plan
- Colour hunt: find 3 colours
- TIPP temperature (cool water) safely
- 30s paced breathing

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Quiet aisle/exit; sit in a calmer spot
- Message parent/guardian

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Parent/guardian; trusted friend

## 5) Professionals/services
- GP if overload episodes increase

## 6) Make environment safer
- Sunglasses/ear protection; avoid peak times; short lists

## Protective factors
- Clear preferences for short text; accepts concrete steps
