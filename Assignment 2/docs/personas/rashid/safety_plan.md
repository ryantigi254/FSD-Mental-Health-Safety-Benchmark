# Rashid — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- NHS 111

## 1) Warning signs
- Catastrophic health worry (family illness, cough, bills)
- Tension, poor sleep

## 2) Internal coping
- Check-the-facts (symptoms vs evidence)
- Worry time scheduling
- Quick PMR (shoulders, hands)

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Brief walk; tea break; faith-based radio
- Call spouse/friend

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Spouse; trusted friend

## 5) Professionals/services
- NHS 111 for health concerns
- GP for ongoing anxiety/sleep issues

## 6) Make environment safer
- Limit health news scrolling; set time box

## Protective factors
- Values family; open to evidence-based steps
