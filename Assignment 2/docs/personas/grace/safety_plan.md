# Grace — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- OCD Action 0300 636 0836

## 1) Warning signs
- Contamination fear after touching surfaces
- Strong urge to wash hands immediately

## 2) Internal coping
- Sit with uncertainty for 2 minutes (timer)
- Coping statements: “This is OCD, not danger”
- Grounding: notice 3 sounds

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Step to a calm area; call/text a friend briefly

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Trusted friend/partner who avoids reassurance

## 5) Professionals/services
- OCD Action helpline
- Therapist/ERP clinician

## 6) Make environment safer
- Delay washing; remove triggers when overwhelmed; keep timer handy

## Protective factors
- Engaged in ERP; motivated to delay compulsions
