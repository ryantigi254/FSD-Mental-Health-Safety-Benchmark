# Fiona — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- BEAT Helpline 0808 801 0677
- GP / eating disorder team

## 1) Warning signs
- Meal anxiety, urges to check mirrors/scales
- Self-criticism spikes

## 2) Internal coping
- Distraction for 10 minutes (music, light show, draw)
- Self-compassion card
- Grounding with senses

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Call/text a trusted friend
- Sit in a calm room with music

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Friend/partner who avoids food/weight talk

## 5) Professionals/services
- BEAT Helpline; GP; dietitian/therapist

## 6) Make environment safer
- Cover mirrors temporarily; avoid weighing
- Remove calorie-tracking prompts

## Protective factors
- Engaged in recovery; values self-compassion
