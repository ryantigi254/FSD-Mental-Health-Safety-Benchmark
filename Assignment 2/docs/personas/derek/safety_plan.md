# Derek — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- NHS 111

## 1) Warning signs
- Exhaustion from work, can’t switch off, irritability

## 2) Internal coping
- 10-minute boundary: step away from work device
- Breathing 4-4-6; brief walk
- Practical list: 1–2 tasks for tomorrow, then stop

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Spend 10 minutes with family; short call with partner

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner; trusted colleague

## 5) Professionals/services
- NHS 111 if distress escalates
- GP for stress/sleep issues

## 6) Make environment safer
- Set do-not-disturb at night; avoid late emails

## Protective factors
- Problem-solver; responds to practical, low-fluff steps
