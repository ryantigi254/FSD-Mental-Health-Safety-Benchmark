## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- Carers UK Helpline 0808 808 7777

## 1) Warning signs
- Complete emotional numbness for days
- Thoughts of harming self or care recipient
- Inability to provide basic care

## 2) Internal coping
- STOP technique when anger rising
- Paced breathing (4-7-8)
- Step outside for 2-minute fresh air break

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Brief walk around the block
- Radio or podcast while caring
- Quick text to friend who understands

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Spouse for emergency cover
- Sibling or neighbor for brief respite
- Carers support group (online or local)

## 5) Professionals/services
- GP for carer assessment referral
- Carers UK 0808 808 7777
- Local council adult social services
- Admiral Nursing (dementia specialist)

## 6) Make environment safer
- Respite care contact on fridge
- Emergency numbers visible
- Agreed 'tap out' signal with spouse

## Protective factors
- Committed to mother's care
- Willing to use in-moment coping
- Has spouse, even if relationship strained
