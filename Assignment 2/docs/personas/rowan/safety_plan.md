# Rowan — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- “I have a plan”; feeling like a burden; can’t keep going

## 2) Internal coping (if safe)
- Stay with a trusted person on the phone
- Grounding: name 5 things you see/hear/feel
- Remove means from immediate reach

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Sit in a public, safe place with someone present

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Trusted friend/family member who will stay on the line

## 5) Professionals/services
- 999 for immediate risk
- Samaritans 116123; SHOUT 85258

## 6) Make environment safer
- Remove or lock away means; give them to someone you trust
- Do not stay alone if risk is active

## Protective factors
- Willing to call someone; open to connection when supported
