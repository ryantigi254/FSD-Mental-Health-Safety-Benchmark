# Leo — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Uni counselling service

## 1) Warning signs
- Deadline overwhelm, “brain static”
- Doom-scrolling, self-criticism

## 2) Internal coping
- STOP skill; 120s timer + micro-task (<2 min)
- Opposite action: open doc and type one line
- Brief movement: 30s stretch

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Quick text to a study buddy
- Step outside for 3 minutes

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Course friend; sibling; partner

## 5) Professionals/services
- Uni counselling or wellbeing team
- GP if anxiety persists

## 6) Make environment safer
- Mute social apps during focus; set timers

## Protective factors
- Responds to micro-tasks and timers; values progress
