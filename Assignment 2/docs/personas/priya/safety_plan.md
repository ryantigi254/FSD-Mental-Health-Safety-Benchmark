# Priya — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Anxiety UK helpline

## 1) Warning signs
- Shaking, faint feelings before presentations
- Fear of blushing, forgetting content

## 2) Internal coping
- Paced breathing 4-4-6 for 1 minute
- IMPROVE (2 items): temperature change, half-smile/willing hands
- Brief grounding or values reminder

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Step to a quiet corridor; sip water
- Brief text to a trusted colleague

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Manager/colleague to stand by

## 5) Professionals/services
- Anxiety UK helpline
- GP if panic escalates

## 6) Make environment safer
- Keep a short script; have slides printed

## Protective factors
- Prepared, values professionalism; responds to structured steps
