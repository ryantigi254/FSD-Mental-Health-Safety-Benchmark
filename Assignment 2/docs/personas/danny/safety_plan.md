# Danny — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Drinkline 0300 123 1110
- Sponsor (phone)

## 1) Warning signs
- Strong evening cravings; shame about past drinking

## 2) Internal coping
- Urge surfing; ice water; paced breathing
- Play the tape forward (tomorrow’s feeling)
- 10-minute distraction (doodle, tidy desk)

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Call sponsor; text friend; step outside briefly

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Sponsor; trusted friend/family

## 5) Professionals/services
- Drinkline; GP; local recovery group

## 6) Make environment safer
- Remove alcohol at home; avoid pubs; plan alternative activity

## Protective factors
- In early recovery; motivated; has sponsor
