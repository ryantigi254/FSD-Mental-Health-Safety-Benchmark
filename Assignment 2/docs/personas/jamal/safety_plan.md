# Jamal — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (medical emergency)
- NHS 111

## 1) Warning signs
- Racing heart on crowded trains
- Fuzzy vision, impending doom

## 2) Internal coping
- Paced breathing 4-4-6 (count out loud)
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding
- Short self-talk: “This is panic, not danger”

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Step off the train to a quieter platform
- Call/text a trusted friend briefly

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner / close friend for a 2-minute call

## 5) Professionals/services
- NHS 111 for medical reassurance
- GP follow-up if panic persists

## 6) Make environment safer
- Sit near doors; carry water; noise-cancelling if possible

## Protective factors
- Knows breathing helps; wants concise support
