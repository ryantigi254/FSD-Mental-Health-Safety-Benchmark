# Aisha — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- “Don’t want to be here” thoughts
- Heavy mornings, lonely nights, lack of structure

## 2) Internal coping
- Gentle paced breathing
- 1-minute task (stand, sip water, open window)
- Text a friend with a short “I need company” note

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Friend/flatmate for a brief chat
- Safe corner with music/blanket

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Close friend who responds fast
- Family member who can stay on the phone

## 5) Professionals/services
- Samaritans 116123; SHOUT 85258
- GP / mental health team if mood drops

## 6) Make environment safer
- Remove or lock away means; keep crisis numbers visible

## Protective factors
- Responds to breathing + connection; willing to reach out
