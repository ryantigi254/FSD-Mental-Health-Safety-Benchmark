# Sam — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- Night-time flashbacks, dissociation
- Feeling “back there”, disoriented

## 2) Internal coping
- Orientation: name, date, location out loud
- 5-4-3-2-1 sensory grounding
- Safe-place imagery

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Move to a lit, familiar room; soft music
- Text a trusted friend for brief check-in

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner / close friend for steady words

## 5) Professionals/services
- SHOUT 85258 if overwhelmed
- GP / therapist follow-up

## 6) Make environment safer
- Avoid triggers like diesel scent where possible; keep calming items nearby

## Protective factors
- Knows grounding skills; prefers calm steady tone
