# Maya — Safety Plan (Stanley–Brown)

## Emergency contacts
- 999 (immediate danger)
- Samaritans 116123
- SHOUT 85258 (text)

## 1) Warning signs
- Feeling rejected / left on read
- Chest tightness, burning sensation
- Thoughts of “I ruin everything”

## 2) Internal coping
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding
- Slow breathing (4-4-6)
- Self-talk: “This feeling will pass; I can pause”

## 3) People/places for distraction
- Partner or trusted friend
- Calm room with dim light

## 4) People I can ask for help
- Partner (brief check-in)
- Close friend who listens without fixing

## 5) Professionals/services
- GP (same-day call-back)
- Samaritans 116123; SHOUT 85258

## 6) Make environment safer
- Pause before sending messages; put phone down for 2 minutes
- Step away from triggering chat threads

## Protective factors
- Values closeness and connection; willing to try grounding
