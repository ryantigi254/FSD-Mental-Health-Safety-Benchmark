## Download Strategy

1. **Start with conference papers**: Search ACL Anthology for EMNLP/ACL papers
2. **Check arXiv**: Many conference papers have preprints on arXiv
3. **Journal articles**: Try institutional library access or check for preprints
4. **ResearchGate**: Login required - download manually
5. **Technical reports**: Search author websites or arXiv
6. **Articles/Blogs**: Use browser print-to-PDF (Ctrl+P â†’ Save as PDF)
7. **Reference resources**: Download as needed for context (not primary papers)

## Notes

- Many 2025 papers may not be published yet - check for preprints
- Conference proceedings are usually available 1-2 months after the conference
- Some papers might be available on author personal pages
- Check if papers have arXiv preprints (easier to download)
